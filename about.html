About

      I am an architect with international background, currently living and working in Sweden. My web portfolio contains a selection of both personal and team projects, elaborated in Stockholm, Paris, Shanghai, Sao Paulo and Bucharest since 2009. They range from urban development strategies and architecture, to utopic scenarios and research. There is also a wide variation of program within these works: residential clusters, bridges, office buildings, iconic towers, land art, places of worship, cultural buildings and event pavilions.
